A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

Hello beauties! We are the creators (Natalia & Arianna) of Celestial Haze Candles. We both have a love for candles, so we decided we were going to make a business out of it! Our business has been up ad running for about a year and a half now. Our candles are 100% soy wa and cruelty free. We aslo hand pick and purchase our crystals individually so each one is special. We love what we do, and put so much time and effort into our candles. However, since we are a small business, we haven't been able to rach as any people as we had hoped to. We want people to enjoy our candles as much as we do. As well as hear what our customers would like to see more of. We are always looking for new ideas and ways to improve our candles. We thank each and everyone of your for visiting our store, and hope you would support our small business by following us on our socials or purchasing one or two of our yummy smelling candles!
