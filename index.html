CELESTIAL HAZE CANDLES

ABOUT

Celestial Haze Candles are made with 100% All Natural Soy and hand poured in small batches.
We use only the finest ingrediants to make these beautiful fragrant candles.
Each candle is infused with dried herbs, and comes with a crystal that we hand pick specifically for each scent.

Featured Scents from left to right: BIRTHDAY CAKE, VAMPIRES BLOOD,PUMPKIN KING, AND WITHCES BREW (HALLOWEEN SCENTS)

As of now, we are only selling 8 ounce candles.
We know the 8 ounces may appear small but have a burn time average of 48 hours.
We hope to make bigger and smaller options for our customers in the near future!
